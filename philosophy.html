Our Story

A Letter From Our CEO

Dear LUMEVA family,

When I founded LUMEVA, I had a simple but deeply personal belief: that every person in Kenya deserves access to the world's finest skincare — not someday, but today.

The beauty industry had long treated us as an afterthought. Shelves stocked with products not designed for our climate, our skin tones, or our daily realities. I wanted to change that. LUMEVA was built to bring the precision of Korean skincare science, the elegance of Japanese beauty ritual, and the richness of Gulf fragrance heritage directly to your doorstep.

Every product on our platform is hand-selected, authenticated, and chosen because we believe it delivers. We don't stock fillers. We don't work with brands whose standards don't meet ours. When you order from LUMEVA, you receive exactly what the label says — and exactly what your skin deserves.

Our team is small, passionate, and deeply invested in your skin journey. We are not just a store — we are your partners in building a ritual that works.

Thank you for trusting us with your skin.

Faris Ismail
CEO & Founder, LUMEVA
